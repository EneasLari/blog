Changing my diet to vegeterian: after 4 months

I decided that I want to change, and I started from what I eat

Posted by Eneas Lari on December 8, 2020

In this article I want to discuss about my decision to change my diet and what happened to my body. I will express my personal opinion on this so if you feel offended please get the f* out of here.

Before 4 months I decided to change something very important for my life. I decided to stop eating meat , only meat not dairy products. I made this decision for two simple reasons. The first is for ethical reasons and the second is for improving my health. I respect any form of life and any creature deserves to live. I always felt a little hypocrite for eating meat as long as I don't have any health issues that demand eating meat. This doesn't mean that everyone who eats meats doesn't love animals or whatever,but you got the point . Next I will refer the benefits and disadvantages I observed in my body.

Benefits

As I was eating a lot of meat my whole life the first month was difficult for me. I have been around 100kg since I was 18 years old. For many years I was eating a lot and I was working out so I Was balancing my body's look, but I always wanted a more lean body. After I removed meat from my diet I saw a drastic change in my body. I am now 8kgs less with the same muscle mass, (yes I didn't became a skinny little bitch!) but I keep the same workout routine.

The big amount of meat I was eating made me feel that my energy level was low. Always that was surprising to me because it should be the opposite. For that reason I was afraid that if I stop eating meat it will make me feel even more exhausted. After one month my energy is higher than before even if I my hunger feeling is more tense. My body is more "light", it is not stuffed and the feeling of heavy breathing has gone away.
Also because I have some stress-related skin problems , I had to reduce fat consumption. So eating meat doesn't help. After four months the skin related problems are in a good condition. Although my stress related issues this period are high. They are not cured because the root of the problem is my stress. I wont mention the other medical benefits of removing the meat from your diet. You can google them and also I am not a doctor my self. But I assure you the benefits are many!

Disadvantages

One of the biggest challenges of not eating meat is finding What to eat. OK now what I will eat with my rice or with my pasta. Solo rice or pasta is too plain. I love cooking, but I didnt know many recipes without meat. So I started googling. I started experimenting with mushrooms(not that kind of mushroom you pot head). Also broccoli, beans ,spinach and a lot of eggs became my everyday meals. As I mentioned eggs, I consume around 30 eggs(sorry chickens around the world) a week. For me is too much but this amount isn't too much for my health. After all this time as a vegetarian, the cooking became one of my favorite things of the day.

Another thing that I don't like as a non meat eater is the feeling of hunger. Is a little difficult to maintain your hunger level low without meat. But that is not a thing that makes you impractical of doing other things. After a while you will be able to control your hunger and even forget about it,so it wont be an obstacle in your routine. I mention that because no one has the time of being around veggies all the time when he/she feels hungry,unless you are a farmer or golf player(who loves grass...). So feeling hungry sometimes makes you unable to think clear about other stuff. And the last thing that i have observed, is that my stomach is more prone to my anxiety and spicy things than before. I am not sure about this as I should see a doctor about this.

My experience from not eating meat is positive with its downsides. But every drastic change like this needs some change time. I don't regret my decision because it makes me feel proud of my self. And also it has helped me to achieve my body goals.

Keep in mind that I am not a doctor and I would not recommend someone to stop eating meat. First be sure that there is no medical issue
